FAMILY WEEKEND CAMPING TICKETS

Family weekend tickets permit access into the designated Family Campsite. This ticket allows entry for one adult and up to two children under the age of 12. Children aged 12 years and under must be pre-registered prior to the event.
Those wishing to stay in the Family Campsite must be accompanied by children 12 years or younger. No single or groups of adults without children will be admitted entry. The Family campsites are restricted in space and therefore must be reserved solely for families with children. Extended family and relatives with no children must camp in general campsites.Please note that ID checks will be in place at the festival, if you feel your child looks older than 12 please bring ID to back this up (photocopies will suffice).

DISABLED ACCESS AND CARERS TICKETS

The process in regards to purchasing your accessible ticket for Electric Picnic has changed for 2019, please see details below:
Disabled Access Customers
Please buy a standard ticket for however long you want to attend the festival as we do not have a disabled customer ticket.
Personal Assistant Ticket (PA)
Once purchased, if you require a PA ticket then you must apply for this via the Disabled Access application form available via the website.
Please note: There is no charge for a Personal Assistant ticket, this ticket can only be issued and approved by the Access team, for more information please refer to the information on our website: ACCESS INFORMATION
Please do not buy a ticket for your PA as we can’t refund this.

CAMPERVAN AND CARAVAN TICKETS

All customers will be sent a sticker to go on their windscreen and will be unable to gain admittance without this sticker, a booking confirmation page is not enough to gain entry. All campervan occupants must have weekend camping tickets to gain access the campervan area. Please note for campervan/caravan passes the purchaser will be required to bring the original proof of purchase to site along with their pass. This is to prevent touting of campervan passes.
Due to a number of campervan/caravan passes going missing in the past, we will now be charging €15 extra for a secure delivery method to ensure tickets are delivered to the original purchaser.
Please ensure you select the correct ticket for the type of vehicle you will be bringing into the festival site: the options are campervan or caravan + car. These tickets are not interchangeable on site.
Separate fields for ticket holders to park campervans and caravans are provided within the festival site.
These fields are split into two sections -
Family Campervan/Caravan + Car only available with family camping tickets and you must specify this when buying your family campervan ticket so that the correct routing sticker can be sent to you.
Standard Campervan/Caravan + Car only available with standard weekend tickets and you must specify this when buying your campervan tickets so that the correct routing stickers can be sent to you.
Phone and Internet bookings are subject to 12.5% service charge per ticket (Max €6.10)
Please ensure you have read our Campervan and Caravan Terms and Conditions.
